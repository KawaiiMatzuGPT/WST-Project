Juniper Chinensis Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Juniperus chinensis

Origin: East Asia (mainly China and Japan)

Description: Known for its fine, dense foliage and rugged bark, Juniper Chinensis is a popular outdoor bonsai prized for its aged appearance and hardy nature.

History: A favorite in classical bonsai aesthetics, especially in Japan where it is often used in formal upright and slanting styles.

Care Tips:

  • Full sun exposure
  • Allow soil to dry slightly between waterings
  • Responds well to shaping and wiring
  • Protect from frost in colder climates
