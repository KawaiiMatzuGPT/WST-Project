Jade Plant Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Crassula ovata

Origin: South Africa and Mozambique

Description: The Jade Plant is a succulent bonsai known for its thick, shiny leaves and sturdy trunk, making it ideal for indoor growth.

History: Often called the "money tree" in Feng Shui, it's a symbol of luck and prosperity and has been cultivated worldwide as a low-maintenance bonsai.

Care Tips:

  • Plenty of sunlight
  • Allow soil to dry between waterings
  • Minimal pruning required
  • Use well-draining soil
