Chinese Juniper Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Juniperus chinensis

Origin: East Asia (China, Japan, Korea)

Description: Chinese Juniper is a hardy and evergreen bonsai known for its fine foliage, textured bark, and iconic deadwood styling techniques like jin and shari.

History: Highly regarded in traditional Japanese bonsai for centuries, often styled into dramatic and natural forms.

Care Tips:

  • Full sunlight
  • Allow soil to dry slightly between watering
  • Prune for shape regularly
  • Protect from frost
