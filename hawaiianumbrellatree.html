Hawaiian Umbrella Tree Bonsai

Scientific Name: Schefflera arboricola

Origin: Tropical Asia and Australia

Description: This low-maintenance bonsai features glossy green leaves arranged in an umbrella-like shape. It's a favorite for indoor settings due to its adaptability.

History: While not traditionally used in Japanese bonsai, it has gained popularity worldwide for beginners and indoor bonsai growers.

Care Tips:

  • Indirect sunlight
  • Keep soil moist but not soggy
  • Prune to maintain compact shape
  • Protect from cold drafts
