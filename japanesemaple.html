Japanese Maple Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Acer palmatum

Origin: Japan, Korea, China

Description: Known for its stunning foliage that changes colors with the seasons, the Japanese Maple is one of the most elegant and popular bonsai trees worldwide.

History: Cultivated in Japan for centuries, it's admired in both traditional and modern bonsai styles for its delicate leaves and graceful form.

Care Tips:

  • Bright, indirect sunlight
  • Water when soil is slightly dry
  • Protect from extreme heat and frost
  • Prune in late spring or early summer
