Gardenia Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Gardenia jasminoides

Origin: Asia (mainly China and Japan)

Description: Gardenia bonsai trees are prized for their creamy white, fragrant blossoms and glossy green foliage, creating a delightful visual and aromatic presence.

History: The Gardenia has been cultivated for centuries in East Asia and symbolizes purity, love, and refinement in traditional cultures.

Care Tips:

  • Bright light with some direct sun
  • Keep soil evenly moist
  • Prefers acidic, well-draining soil
  • Protect from drafts and temperature extremes
