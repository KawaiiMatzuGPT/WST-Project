European Beech Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Fagus sylvatica

Origin: Europe

Description: The European Beech Bonsai is admired for its smooth gray bark, elegant leaves, and seasonal color changes. It forms a refined silhouette with careful pruning.

History: European Beech has been used in bonsai across Europe due to its graceful branching and tolerance for shaping.

Care Tips:

  • Full sun to partial shade
  • Keep soil moist but well-drained
  • Protect from extreme cold
  • Prune in early spring or mid-summer
