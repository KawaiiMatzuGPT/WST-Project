Carmona Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Ehretia microphylla

Origin: Southeast Asia

Description: Carmona, also known as Fukien Tea, is a small-leaved tree with shiny foliage and tiny white flowers, perfect for indoor bonsai lovers.

History: Popular among beginner and indoor growers due to its forgiving care requirements and year-round beauty.

Care Tips:

  • Bright, indirect light
  • Keep slightly moist
  • Trim regularly
  • Avoid cold drafts
