Juniper Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Juniperus

Origin: Northern Hemisphere

Description: Juniper bonsai trees are iconic for their hardy nature and graceful, twisted forms. They are often shaped using wire and develop distinct character with age.

History: Junipers have long been popular in traditional bonsai practices in both Japan and China. They symbolize strength and longevity.

Care Tips:

  • Full sun exposure
  • Allow soil to dry between waterings
  • Regular pruning to shape
  • Protect from extreme cold
