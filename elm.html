Elm Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Ulmus

Origin: Northern Hemisphere (Europe, Asia, and North America)

Description: Elm bonsai trees are known for their small, serrated leaves and strong, resilient structure, making them ideal for shaping and styling.

History: Elm trees have been cultivated for ornamental purposes for centuries and are favored in bonsai for their graceful branches and seasonal color changes.

Care Tips:

  • Bright indirect sunlight
  • Allow soil to dry slightly between watering
  • Prune regularly to maintain shape
  • Protect from extreme frost
