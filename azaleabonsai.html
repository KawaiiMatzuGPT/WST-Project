Azalea Bonsai Tree

Scientific Name: Rhododendron

Origin: Asia (mainly Japan and China)

Description: The Azalea Bonsai is known for its breathtaking flowers that bloom in spring. It's favored for its bright color and artistic form.

History: Grown in Japan for centuries, Azaleas have deep cultural significance and are a staple in traditional Japanese bonsai exhibitions.

Care Tips:

  • Partial sunlight
  • Keep soil moist
  • Prune after flowering
  • Use acidic bonsai soil
